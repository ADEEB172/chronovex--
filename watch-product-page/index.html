Description

The transparent black dial of this diving watch features gray printed indexes, white printed numerals, black hour and minute hands, and a gray seconds hand, all with glow-in-the-dark effect, under biosourced glass. A matte black and matte transparent black strap with a matte black loop and shiny, transparent black biosourced buckle complete the watch.
